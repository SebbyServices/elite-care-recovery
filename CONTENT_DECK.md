## Product: NICE1 Cold + Compression System

(From the NICE brochure — Elite Care is a **distributor**, not the manufacturer.)

**Tagline:** Engineered for the Comeback.

**Product description:** NICE uses advanced technology to greatly improve the convenience of cold + compression therapy. NICE eliminates inconvenience by delivering precise cold therapy without the need for ice. The programmable pneumatic compression is proven to reduce edema and speed recovery. NICE integrates these highly effective therapies in a small (8x8x8 inches) and lightweight (9lb) package with an easy-to-use touch screen interface.

**Key Features:**
- No need to add water — once technician sets up the unit, patients don't worry about ice or water
- Consistent temperature control: 42°F to 58°F
- Touch-screen display for cold + compression control
- Timed programmability for overnight use
- Form-fitting wraps designed specifically for each joint
- Compact (8x8x8 inches), lightweight (9 lbs)

**Benefits:**
- **Compliance** — patients are much more compliant when no ice/water is required
- **Faster healing** — decreases edema, improves lymphatic drainage, improves ROM
- **Mobility** — easier to transport than traditional ice machines
- **Temperature control** — accommodates different patient sensitivities
- **Safety** — no thermal damage (unit cannot go below 42°F)
- **Personal settings** — pre-set programs for "quick start" and overnight sessions

**Three core selling points (use as 3-up icon grid on homepage):**
1. **Iceless** — Patients don't need to add ice to the unit
2. **Cold + Compression** — Static or intermittent compression at low/medium/high levels
3. **Opioid Reduction** — Immediate pain relief, reduces pain medication intake
